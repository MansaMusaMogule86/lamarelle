Our Philosophy

Education as a
living experience

We draw from the Reggio Emilia approach, Waldorf sensibilities, and Nordic outdoor learning traditions to craft an environment where children are the architects of their own discovery.

Every detail — from the grain of our wooden furniture to the seasonal rotation of our library — is intentional. We believe the environment is the third teacher.
