The Campus

Where curiosity finds its home in every corner.

A sanctuary of natural light, tactile textures, and boundless inspiration designed for the early years.

our Building

On the ground floor, you will find the reception area, classrooms, and the library, along with a dedicated playroom for children aged 3 months to 3 years.

On the first floor, you will find the kindergarten classrooms and a dedicated motor skills room for children aged 3 to 5 years.

Neurosciences

In addition to the educational requirements imposed by the Ministry, we rely heavily on neuroscience research to complement our educational methodologies.

The Dolto, Freinet and Montessori methods have shown a clear link between a child's environment, emotional climate and the development of key areas of the brain. We integrate the teachings of these pedagogies into our daily practices.

Our Little Extras

Motor Skills Room

Used daily by all six classes for motor development activities, dance initiation, gymnastics, and yoga, fostering coordination and physical confidence.

Playroom

A space designed to nurture children's independence, creativity, and social bonds. It supports various forms of play, including role-playing and hands-on activities.

Library

A haven where books enrich children's language and vocabulary skills. Here, they learn to express themselves, comprehend others, and explore new ideas.
