Teacher Observations

Oct 12, 2023

Observation by Ms. Clara

Moment of Empathy

Leo showed great empathy today by sharing his favourite wooden blocks with a new student who was feeling a bit shy. He calmly explained how to build a "skyscraper" and stayed by their side for the duration of free-play. It was a beautiful display of emotional maturity.

Sep 28, 2023

Observation by Mr. David

Early Literacy Spark

During reading time, Leo correctly identified all letter sounds in his own name and attempted to trace them in the sand tray. He is showing a keen interest in the relationship between symbols and sounds, often asking "What does this letter say?" when looking at picture books.
